If you spot a snake

Stay Calm. Here's What To Do.

1
Keep eyes on it

Have someone maintain a safe visual on the snake from a distance. Snakes will typically find a hiding spot or run once alone.

2
Do not approach it

Keep pets and children away. Never attempt to handle, trap, or use sticky glue traps — these cause unnecessary harm.

3
Call or text us

Reach us at (512) 626-3864. Text is preferred. We'll give you guidance and dispatch as soon as possible.

4
We'll handle the rest

A specialist arrives to safely relocate the snake. No harm, no drama — just a calmer yard.
